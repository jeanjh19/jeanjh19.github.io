Machine Learning - Avito Prediction

Project working with Yicheng Zhang

Project Description

Our project is to predict the deal probability (or the probability of selling a product successfully) of online advertisements of used products based on data such as title, description and image. We firstly extracted features from texts and images. Bag of words and text statistical features are used as the representation of textual data. Five features such as blurriness and white- ness which reflect image quality were extracted from images. Truncated SVD was used for feature reduction. Three models: Light GBM, Multilayer Perceptron, and Ridge Regression were used to predict the deal probability. We designed three experiments to explore which model is the best and what features are important.
